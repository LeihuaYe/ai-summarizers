## This Week's Highlight
**Holistic Evaluation and Failure Diagnosis of AI Agents**

This paper delivers a striking empirical result: the same frontier model achieves 3.5× better failure localization simply by changing evaluation methodology from monolithic judging to a structured top-down/bottom-up approach. For practitioners building or evaluating agentic systems, this means the bottleneck to understanding agent failures is likely your eval framework, not the underlying model. Combined with the memory-safety paper showing that temporal context is critical for safety evals, this week underscores that robust agent evaluation requires both structural rigor and longitudinal coverage — neither of which today's standard benchmarks provide.
