## This Week's Highlight

**Meta Llama 4: Natively Multimodal MoE Arrives as Open Weight**

Meta's Llama 4 release is the most consequential open-weight event of the week — and arguably of 2026 so far. For practitioners, the implications are immediate: Scout (10M token context, single-H100 deployable) and Maverick (128-expert MoE, outperforming GPT-4o on several benchmarks) are freely downloadable today. The shift to native multimodality via early fusion means vision is no longer a bolt-on capability — it is trained end-to-end with language from the start. For teams building evaluation pipelines or fine-tuning workflows, this is a new baseline to test against; early independent evals show mixed coding results relative to specialized models, making benchmark choice especially important when assessing Llama 4 for your use case.
