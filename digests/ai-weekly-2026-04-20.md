## This Week's Highlight

**Google DeepMind's Tracked Capability Levels: Proactive Safety Infrastructure**

DeepMind's TCL framework is the most structurally significant safety release this week for practitioners who build or evaluate frontier models. Rather than waiting for a model to cross a hard "Critical Capability Level" threshold, TCLs create a monitoring layer that tracks emerging capabilities *before* they become critical — effectively instrumenting the path to dangerous capabilities rather than just the endpoint. For teams doing capability evaluations or red-teaming, this is a concrete reference architecture for how a major lab is operationalizing continuous safety assessment at scale, and it sets a precedent other labs are likely to follow.
